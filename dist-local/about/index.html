About OpenCS

OpenCS is an open-source visual learning platform designed specifically for BTech CSE students in India. Our mission is to make computer science fundamentals accessible, intuitive, and free.

Our Mission

Every BTech CSE student in India deserves access to high-quality, visual learning resources. OpenCS bridges the gap between theoretical concepts and intuitive understanding through interactive visualizations powered by reusable engines.

Design Philosophy

We believe in textbook-modern design — clean, academic, and focused on clarity. No flashy animations or startup SaaS aesthetics. Just clear explanations, interactive widgets, and step-by-step examples that work.

Architecture

OpenCS is built on four reusable engines that power all visualizations:

  • Sequence Engine — Linear operations: sorting, recursion, time complexity
  • TreeGraph Engine — Hierarchical structures: trees, BSTs, graphs
  • Theory Engine — Formal systems: DFA/NFA, automata
  • SystemProcess Engine — OS simulations: CPU scheduling

Open Source

Licensed under MIT, OpenCS welcomes contributions from educators, developers, and students. Every contribution follows our pedagogy standards to ensure consistent educational quality.

Technology

Built with Astro for static generation, React for interactive components, and MDX for content. Deployed to GitHub Pages for fast, free access to anyone.
